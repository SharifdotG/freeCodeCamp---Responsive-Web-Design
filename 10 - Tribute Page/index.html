Ada Lovelace

The First Computer Programmer

Here's a timeline of Ada Lovelace's life:

  • 1815 - Born in London, England
  • 1833 - Met Charles Babbage, the inventor of the first mechanical computer
  • 1842 - Translated an article on Babbage's Analytical Engine, adding her own notes which contained the first algorithm intended to be processed by a machine
  • 1843 - Published her notes on the Analytical Engine, which included the first computer program
  • 1852 - Died at the age of 36

"That brain of mine is something more than merely mortal; as time will show."

-- Ada Lovelace
